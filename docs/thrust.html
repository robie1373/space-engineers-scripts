This tool provides the calculations necessary to ensure your vessel is flightworthy. You can simulate the atmospheres and gravity wells of different planets while planning a drop ship; calculate the thrust needed for a ship to overcome a planet's gravity; measure the amount of hydrogen needed for a stable orbit; estimate the amount of cargo weight an existing ship can carry for a given planet.
